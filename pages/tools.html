# Arab teams name A brief Description
1 Qatar Qatar's national team played its first FIFA World Cup qualifying match in 1977. The national team came closest to qualifying for the World Cup in the 1990s, when the team reached the final stages of two World Cup qualifications in 1990 and 1998.
2 Saudi Arabia Competing in it's sixth FIFA World Cup and second in a row, Saudi Arabia is ranked at 49 on the FIFA Men's World Ranking. The nickname of the Saudi Arabian men's national team are Al-Suqour (The Falcons) and Al-Akhdhar (The Green).
3 Tunisia In their first appearance in the 1978 World Cup, Tunisia shocked the world when they managed to defeat Mexico 3-1 and draw with Germany before being eliminated from the tournament after the first round.
4 Morocco Their best performance to date was in the 1986 World Cup where they reached the round of 16. At that years tournament in Mexico, they managed to defeat Portugal by 3-1. During the 2018 World Cup in Russia, the Atlas Lions managed to draw 2-2 with Spain in the group finale.
